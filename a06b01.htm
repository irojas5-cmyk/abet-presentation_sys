Feedback-driven and/or assessment-validated program changes since the 2014 ABET accreditation cycle

  1. Addition of ChE 425 (Nanotechnology for Pharmaceutical Applications) as a technical elective.

  2. Addition of ChE 451 (Renewable Energy Technologies) as a technical elective.

  3. Incorporation of a process control experiment into the CHE 381/382 laboratory curriculum

  4. Improvement to extracurricular student learning experiences in Career Development, Industry/Student Networking, Alumni/Student interactions
    • Engineering Career Center/ChE department hosted "ChE Industry Day"
    • ChemE Forum with alumni/student networking
    • Chemical Engineering LinkedIn group with students and alumni

  5. Improvement of the ethics content in the curriculum
    • ChE 396/397- Senior Design I and II
    • ChE 499 Professional Development Seminar

  6. Ongoing improvement to the safety content in the curriculum
    • ChE 381 - Chemical Engineering Laboratory I
    • ChE 382 - Chemical Engineering Laboratory II
    • ChE 396 - Senior Design I
    • ChE 397 - Senior Design II

  7. Improvement to the ENGR100/ENGR101 Chemical Engineering Breakout session

  8. Future Improvements to the Curriculum: New Concentrations to the BS Major
    • Concentration in Polymers and Molecular Engineering
    • Concentration in Energy and Environment
    • Concentration in Nanotechnology
    • Concentration in Process Automation
    • Concentration in Entrepreneurship

  9. Future Improvements to the Curriculum: New courses aligned with the concentrations
    • CHE 230 Molecular Systems in Chemical Engineering
    • CHE 330 Polymer Science
    • CHE 454 Molecular and Macromolecular Engineering
    • CHE 455 Nanoscale Systems in Chemical Engineering
    • CHE 457: Colloidal and Interfacial Phenomena
