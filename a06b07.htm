ChE 210 - Material and Energy Balances
UIC Course Description Material and energy balances applied to chemical systems. Introduction to chemical and physical properties. Introduction to the use of computers for chemical process calculations. Prerequisite(s): Credit or concurrent registration in CHE 201; and credit or concurrent registration in CHE 205.
