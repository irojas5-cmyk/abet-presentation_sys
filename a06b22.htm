ChE 494 - Two-Dimensional (2D) Nanomaterials: Science, Processing, and Applications
UIC Course Description The goal of this course is to prepare students with foundational understanding of advanced two-dimensional (2D) nanomaterials including graphene, and the tools required to characterize suchatomically-thin materials, and their potential applications.
