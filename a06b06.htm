ChE 205 - Computational Methods in Chemical Engineering
UIC Course Description Computational methods and software relevant to unit operations. Excel spreadsheets (curve fitting, heat conduction), Matlab, Aspen Plus (process simulation), algorithms and object oriented concepts in chemical engineering. Prerequisite(s): Credit or concurrent registration in CHE 201 and MATH 210.
